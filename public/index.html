We Give You The Tools For Success:

Physical Thereapists aren't only available to provide treatment and send you on your way, they are available to educate you. Proper education about why you've been prescribed a specific treatment, and lessons on how to continue it when you're out of the clinic puts much of your success in your own hands.


Every treatment, no matter the ailment, requires effort on the part of our patients. When you arrive, your appointment will consist of a assessment, diagnois, physical exam and a possible start to treatment


From this point, you will put in the work at the clinic with the help of your therapist, and continue any excericses assigned throughout the week at home on your own. If this proves difficult, just call your therapist before your next appointment and they'll be happy to give you tips and pointers!


Our therapists love to set the path, but it's your energy that will carry you to the finish line.

Preventative And Focused Treatment:

You can benefit from the physiotherapy at any time in your life - whether you're old or young, injured or looking to prevent injury.


Focused treatment is deteremined by each individual diagnosis. Through physical and occupationial therapy, the therapists of Robert Foster Inc. are well versed in assiting with recovery after stroke, life with osteoperosis, arthritis and other orthopedic disorders


Focused treatment is applied to short term injuries such as a sprained ankle or hairline fracture. Physiotherapy is important for the treatment of these injuries so that you will be able to perform day to day tasks again with no residual weakness


Preventive treatment is a reason many athletes seek out physiotherapy. Through wducation, fitness prgrams, monility and flexibility excercises, your chances of being injured during a game can be greatly decreased.


For each individual diagnoses, there is an indivial fitness and recovery plan. No two patients are the same our therapist care about each case.
